My initial reactions (Section 1a)

What emotions/feelings does this performance invoke? What is your initial reaction?

My initial reaction was joy. I felt happy because you could hear the singers very clearly, and they were in sync. Even when the lower part got quieter, you could still hear them clearly.

What do you think the purpose of this song is?

I think the purpose of this song was for entertainment. I know Inuit singers perform throat singing as a form of entertainment and contest, to see who can outlast the other.

In what setting do you think the song would be performed?

I think this is a song that would be performed in a friend/family gathering. I think this because throat singing traditionally is less of a music genre, and more of a game.

Where (geographically) do you think this music is from and what made you think it was from this area?

I think this music is from Canada. I know this because there are two people singing, which is how Inuit throat singing is performed.
